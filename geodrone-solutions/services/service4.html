Volume Calculation

Accurate volumetric analysis for mining, construction, and land management

Geodrone Solution offers volume calculation using drone-based 3D modeling and photogrammetry. By capturing high-resolution aerial images and generating 3D point clouds, our team accurately calculates cut and fill volumes for construction sites, quarries, and land development projects.

  • Applications: construction sites, mining, quarries, stockpile measurement, and road projects.
  • Deliverables: 3D point clouds, digital terrain models (DTM), volume reports (PDF/Excel), and contour maps.

Why choose our volume calculation service?

Using high-resolution aerial imagery and 3D reconstruction techniques, we calculate the exact volume of stockpiles and excavations. Our reports allow you to make informed decisions for inventory management, production planning, and compliance documentation.
